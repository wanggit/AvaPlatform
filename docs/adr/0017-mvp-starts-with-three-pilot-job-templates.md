# MVP starts with three Pilot Job Templates

MVP should demonstrate three concrete Pilot Job Templates instead of a broad generic role library: Enterprise Intelligence Employee, Customer Support Ticket Coordinator, and Sales Proposal Collaborator. These cover management analysis, customer service coordination, and sales enablement.

This keeps the product grounded in measurable business scenarios. Each pilot template should have concrete inputs, outputs, acceptance criteria, Goal Risk Level boundaries, Template Evaluation cases, and KPI signals. More templates can be added later after the platform proves value in these three categories.
