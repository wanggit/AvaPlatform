# Goal Risk Level controls automation boundary

MVP introduces Goal Risk Level as a four-level business risk classification for Goals and Goal Runs: L1 information assistance, L2 work coordination, L3 controlled execution, and L4 high-risk decision support. L4 explicitly means AI prepares materials and options, while humans make final decisions and execute final accountable actions.

Goal Risk Level is distinct from Tool risk level. Tool risk describes a callable capability; Goal Risk Level describes the business task. Platform uses both: Job Templates declare the highest Goal Risk Level their Digital Employees may handle, Delegation Policy tightens with higher risk, and Tool Gateway blocks or requires approval based on the combined Goal and Tool risk.
